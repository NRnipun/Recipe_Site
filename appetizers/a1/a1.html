Eggplant Caponata

Savor the flavors of Italy!

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • Red pepper
  • Red onion
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Capers

Instructions:

1. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.

2. Add garlic, red onion, celery, and red pepper. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.

3. Add eggplant and cook until it starts to brown.

4. Pour in canned tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, and sugar. Stir to combine.

5. Add capers for a briny flavor. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.

6. Serve over your favorite pasta, garnished with fresh basil and pine nuts.
